Welcome ,

You have accessed the Computer Resource Center's (CRC) official Student Registration System running FreeSMS. The CRC (hypothetical demonstartion facility) is an education body whose mandate is to educate our community members in technology and technological trends.

The CRC hold various computer related courses througout the year for our community so that our community members can take advantage of trainning at minimal cost (enough to cover operational expenses only). The courses that are run by the CRC fall into the following categories:

  • Operating Systems (such as Microsoft Windows and Linux)
  • Office Applications (such as Microsoft Office and OpenOffice)
  • Development Languages and Tools (such as VB.NET and Java)

CRC members would like to thank all its volunteers (Operational Staff, Teachers and Students) who make the CRC a viable organization.

We hope that you will find the recources and information that you are looking for. For any reason if you have feedback or problems regarding any page or content, please do not hesistate to contact a CRC member.
